About Our Club

The Math & Science Club (MSC) is a student-led organization at the Institut National des Postes et Télécommunications (INPT) in Morocco dedicated to fostering a deep appreciation for the analytical and empirical sciences. Originally established within a community of future developers and engineers, the club has expanded its mission to empower students by bridging the gap between theoretical academic concepts and practical, real-world applications in mathematics and various scientific disciplines. Through a dynamic schedule of hands-on workshops, internal training sessions, and high-level competitions, the MSC provides a collaborative environment where members can sharpen their problem-solving skills and engage in innovative research projects. Whether preparing for national engineering challenges like ESPOIR 2026 or hosting intensive coding and logic sessions, the club serves as a premier hub for intellectual growth and scientific excellence within the INPT community.

We organize workshops, internal training sessions, and national coding competitions.
